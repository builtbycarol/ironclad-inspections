Why It Matters

Buying a Home in Prince George Is a Big Decision

Whether you're purchasing your first home in Hart Highlands, upgrading to a larger place in College Heights, or investing in a property near UNBC — a home inspection is the single most important step before you commit.

Common Questions

Before You Book

Do I need to be there for the inspection?

You don't have to be, but Mike strongly recommends attending at least the final 30-minute walkthrough. Seeing things in person makes a big difference — especially for first-time buyers in Prince George who may not be familiar with older home systems.

How quickly can I get booked in?

Usually within 1–2 business days. Real estate in Prince George moves fast, and Mike understands that. Call (250) 649-5522 if you need something urgent.

Can I use the report to negotiate the sale price?

Absolutely — and many buyers do. If Mike identifies significant issues, your realtor can use the findings to negotiate repairs or a price reduction before you close.

What if the house passes the inspection?

Every home has something — no inspection comes back perfectly clean. The goal isn't to pass or fail; it's to give you a clear, honest picture of the property so you can make an informed decision.

Do you serve areas outside Prince George?

Yes. Ironclad covers Quesnel, Vanderhoof, and communities within approximately 1–1.5 hours of Prince George. Not sure if your property qualifies? Call Mike and he'll confirm.
