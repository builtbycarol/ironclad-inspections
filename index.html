The Inspector

Why Ironclad?

Mike Webber brings 15 years of hands-on trades experience to every inspection. After years working in sawmills and around heavy equipment, he wanted something new — and with his wife Tara now working in mortgages and a deep network of realtors across Northern BC, home inspections were a natural fit.

Ironclad's meticulous "Inside & Out" approach is built on three unbreakable pillars: unparalleled accuracy using advanced tools for foundational and structural analysis, detailed professional reports that serve as an ironclad blueprint for your property's health, and high-end, unbiased knowledge that protects your biggest financial investment.

Every inspection ends with a personal walkthrough — Mike dedicates the last 30 minutes to walking you through the property, showing you findings in person, and explaining any issues he's identified. No other inspector in Prince George does this. You'll also receive a comprehensive digital report within 24 hours (often the same evening), with every deficiency linked to a recommended local repair professional so you don't have to search for contractors yourself.

Ironclad Inspections is independently owned — not a franchise. When Mike inspects, he sets The Gold Standard. That means clarity, thoroughness, and straight answers on every job.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

A typical "Inside & Out" inspection for a single-family home takes between 3.5 to 4.5 hours, depending on the size and condition of the property. Mike takes his time to meet The Gold Standard of accuracy, examining everything from the roof to the foundation.

Ironclad follows a strict, comprehensive checklist covering all major home systems: foundation, grading, and roof (structural); siding, windows, doors, and drainage (exterior); plumbing, electrical, HVAC, and attic (interior); plus gas leak detection and visible moisture issues (safety). You'll receive a detailed report with photos covering every area assessed.

While not required, Mike highly recommends it. Attending the final 30 minutes of the inspection allows him to walk you through the property, show you findings in person, and explain any "future headaches" he's identified. It's the best way to get the full Ironclad experience.

Real estate moves fast, and so does Mike. You'll receive your high-resolution, digital Ironclad Report via email within 24 hours of the inspection — often the same evening. Reports are mobile-friendly and include clear photos and easy-to-understand summaries.
